About Me

Hi! I'm Erik, a software developer from Malta, currently living and working in the Netherlands.

I have over 2 years of experience in the software industry, and have worked on several ongoing products and delivered X projects, of which X are on VR platforms. Outside of programming, my hobbies include digital and tabletop gaming, reading, worldbuilding, and music production.

As a programmer, my focus is on growth and expanding my skill-set.
